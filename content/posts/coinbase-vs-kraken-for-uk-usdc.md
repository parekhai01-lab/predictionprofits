If you're in the UK and want to use Polymarket, one of the first decisions is where to buy USDC.

For most people, it comes down to **Coinbase vs Kraken**.

The short version:

- **Coinbase** is better for beginners
- **Kraken** is often better on pricing for larger buys

If you just want the simplest route from GBP to Polymarket, {{< affiliate link="coinbase" text="Coinbase" >}} is usually the easiest place to start. If you care more about tighter execution and you’re buying larger amounts, {{< affiliate link="kraken" text="Kraken" >}} is often the better value.

## Quick Verdict

### Best for beginners
**Coinbase**

Why:
- simpler interface
- easier onboarding
- very straightforward for GBP deposits
- good if you're buying a modest amount and just want to get moving

### Best for larger amounts
**Kraken**

Why:
- tighter spreads in many cases
- stronger fee structure for more active users
- better if you're more comfortable with exchange interfaces

## What Matters for Polymarket Users

You are not choosing an exchange for day trading here. You're choosing the easiest and cheapest route to:

1. deposit **GBP**
2. buy **USDC**
3. withdraw it on **Polygon**
4. send it to your wallet
5. use it on Polymarket

The key factors are:
- ease of use
- GBP deposit options
- spread / effective cost
- Polygon USDC withdrawals
- speed and reliability

## Which Is Better for Small Amounts?

If you're just testing Polymarket with a small amount, the difference is not huge.

For example:
- under £200, convenience matters more than shaving every last basis point
- getting from GBP to USDC quickly is the main objective

That makes {{< affiliate link="coinbase" text="Coinbase" >}} the better default for most first-timers.

## Which Is Better for Larger Amounts?

If you're buying bigger amounts of USDC, Kraken starts to make more sense.

Why:
- small spread differences become meaningful at scale
- more pricing control matters more
- if you're doing repeated funding and withdrawals, better execution compounds over time

So for larger buys, {{< affiliate link="kraken" text="Kraken" >}} usually wins.

## Important Detail: Withdraw on Polygon

Whichever exchange you use, this matters more than almost anything else:

**You need your USDC on Polygon for Polymarket.**

Before confirming any withdrawal:
- check the network carefully
- confirm Polygon is selected
- verify your receiving wallet address

Sending on the wrong chain is one of the most common mistakes beginners make.

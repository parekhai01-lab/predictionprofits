## When a VPN actually helps

A VPN is useful if you:
- travel often
- trade on hotel / airport / café Wi‑Fi
- don’t want every connection tied directly to your home IP
- want a cleaner security setup around trading accounts

It is **not** a substitute for:
- good wallet security
- a hardware wallet
- phishing awareness
- keeping your seed phrase offline
